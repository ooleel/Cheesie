Woodfired, fresh, and Full of flavour

Cheesie is all about good vibes, great food, and even better company. Whether you're here for a quick bite or a laid-back evening with friends, our cozy, chill atmosphere makes every visit special.

Our pizzas are handcrafted with fresh, locally sourced ingredients, cooked to perfection in our traditional woodfire oven for that irresistible smokey flavour. From classic Margherita to bold, gourmet creations, there's something for every pizza lover.

Swing by, grab a slice, and taste the difference. Because pizza this good deserves to be shared!
